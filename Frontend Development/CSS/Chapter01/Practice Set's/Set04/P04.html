Publication History

Premiere


Iron Man was first introduced to the public on October 16, 2008, at the 2008 Academy Awards in New York City.

Solo Series

The film premiered in the United States on November 17, 2008, and in the United Kingdom on November 22, 2008. In the United States, it grossed $27.8 million worldwide, making it the highest-grossing film of 2008.
